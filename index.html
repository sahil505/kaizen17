About Kaizen

Kaizen is a joint initiative of NSS IIT Delhi & PESR, a platform to recognize the continuous improvements that is happening in our society as a result of the combined efforts all socially motivated student bodies and all NGOs across India and abroad.

Interaction and opinion sharing is a prime focal point of Kaizen. It aims at providing a single platform for exchange of a plethora of ideologies, stories & experiences of people who stood up against the tides and sailed through it, so that any idea or solution implemented at one place can also become success model for others.
